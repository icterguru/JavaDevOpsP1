ICTER Guru Journal of Humanities and Social Science

The ICTER Guru Journal of Humanities and Social Science (JHSS) is a peer-reviewed open-access journal that publishes the full text of original research manuscripts, review articles, case studies, and so on. The publications frequency of the journal is quarterly (March, June, September, and December). The journal aims at improving the efforts of researchers and scholars to accomplish goals and objectives by using available resources, efficiently and effectively. The journal will be published in both online and print versions. ICTER Guru Journal Template Considered subject areas include: Anthropology Communication studies Corporate governance Criminology Cross-cultural Studies Demography Development studies Economics Ethics Geography History Human Factor Human Rights Human Right Law Human Resource Development Human Studies Industrial Relations Information Sciences Law Library Science Media Studies Philosophy Political Science Population Studies Psychology Public administration Sociology Social Welfare Performing arts (music, theatre & dance) Religious studies Visual arts Women Studies and relative topics in early findings and case studies on fundamental and applied aspects of educational and management science. ICTER Guru publishes original papers, review papers, conceptual frameworks, analytical and simulation models, case studies, empirical research, technical notes, and book reviews.
